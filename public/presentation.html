The Problem of Warehouse Inefficiency

Disorganized warehouses lead to wasted time, increased labor costs, and slower fulfillment. Finding items or making space becomes a daily struggle. This is where smart placement becomes a game-changer.

Logic: Volume-Based Placement

The core principle is simple: **high-volume products belong closer to the loading gates**. Warehouse Navigator's algorithm analyzes each product's volume to calculate its ideal row, minimizing travel time for your most frequently moved items.

  • High Volume: Placed in lower rows (e.g., Row 1-3) for quick access.
  • Low Volume: Stored in higher rows, further from the gates.

Key Features

  • Interactive Map: A color-coded grid for at-a-glance capacity management.
  • Intelligent Moves: When you move a product, the app highlights the *ideal* cell with a star and suggests nearby empty cells in color-coded zones.
  • Floor Optimization: A one-click button to rearrange an entire floor to its perfect state.
